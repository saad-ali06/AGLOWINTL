About Us

We AGLOW INTERNATIONAL is an International Cargo Movement / Freight Forwarder and Customs Broker since 1996.

Aglow International is a progressive logistics provider based in Lahore (Pakistan). We are committed to delivering efficient, cost-effective freight forwarding and customs clearance solutions through our global network of partners.

Whether by air, sea, or land, our experienced team handles shipments with professionalism and care. From managing documentation to providing warehousing and distribution, we ensure a seamless end-to-end logistics experience for our clients.

We have a diverse client base ranging from Industrial Machinery, Spare Parts, Industrial Raw Material, Fabrics, Textiles, Chemicals, Medical Instruments, Disposables, Advertising Material, Computers, Computer Parts, Electronics, Foodstuff, Perishables, Households, Personal Effects, Cosmetics, Furniture and Miscellaneous Items. We are adept at working under pressure and complying with tight deadlines. You can depend on us to have your goods delivered on time.
